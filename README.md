# PxLink: Smart Pharmacy and Healthcare Management
### Optimizing Medicine Supply Chains through Predictive Analytics and Smart Sourcing

## Overview
PxLink is a specialized pharmaceutical inventory ecosystem designed to eliminate medical waste and ensure life-saving medications are consistently available. By acting as a Virtual Business Analyst, the system analyzes medication data to predict shortages and optimize stock levels, while seamlessly connecting pharmacies with reliable suppliers.

## The Healthcare Challenge
* **Medication Obsolescence:** Significant financial losses occur annually due to expired medications that lack effective tracking.
* **Critical Shortages:** Delays in detecting low stock of essential drugs directly impact patient healthcare and operational readiness.
* **Sourcing Fragmentation:** Manual processes for finding alternative suppliers during shortages lead to inefficiencies in pricing and delivery.

## The Solution
1. **Pharmaceutical Analytics:** Utilizes historical data to identify slow-moving drugs and predict stock-out events for critical medications.
2. **Supplier Integration Engine:** A smart module that connects to supplier databases to automatically retrieve optimal pricing and availability for missing items.

## Technologies Used
* **Frontend:** React.js (Vite)
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **UI Components:** shadcn/ui (Radix UI)
* **Icons:** Lucide React
* **Animation:** Framer Motion
* **ORM:** Prisma
* **Database:** Neon (PostgreSQL)
* **Deployment:** Vercel

# 📦 InventoryHup
### *Bridging the Gap Between Inventory Management and Collaborative Sourcing*

## 🌟 Overview
**InventoryHub** is an intelligent inventory management ecosystem designed to eliminate supply chain waste and optimize stock levels. Unlike traditional systems, SSE acts as a **Virtual Business Analyst**, providing data-driven insights while connecting businesses through a decentralized marketplace to exchange surplus stock.

## ⚠️ The Problem
In the current industrial and commercial landscape, businesses face three major challenges:
- **Invisible Waste:** Surplus inventory remains unmonitored until it expires or becomes obsolete.
- **Reactive Stock-outs:** Shortages are often detected too late, causing operational downtime.
- **Sourcing Inefficiency:** Finding alternatives during a shortage is a manual process that ignores optimal pricing and proximity.

## ✨ The Solution
Inventory solves these issues through a dual-layered approach:
1. **Predictive Analytics:** Analyzes historical data to forecast demand and provide actionable "Business Insights" (e.g., Identifying slow-moving items or predicting imminent shortages).
2. **Collaborative Marketplace:** A secondary platform where users list surplus items. The system's **Smart Matcher** automatically compares external offers (Price vs. Quantity) to suggest the best sourcing option.

## 🏗 System Architecture
The project is built using **Clean Architecture** principles to ensure scalability and separation of concerns.

### 🏛 Layers:
- **Presentation Layer:** React (Vite) with `shadcn/ui` for a high-performance, accessible dashboard.
- **Core Logic Layer:** Dedicated services for **Analytics Processing** and **Price Matching Algorithms**.
- **Data Layer:** `Prisma ORM` coupled with `Neon (PostgreSQL)` for serverless, reliable data persistence.
